# 509 Cedar St / 509 Long Shore Dr (Ann Arbor)

## Facts
- Ann Arbor city directories list multiple Seehafer household members at 509 Cedar (1908-1919).
- Death certificates for William Gottlob Seehafer (1921) and Wilhelmina Seehafer (1922) list residence as 509 Cedar.
- Death certificate for Ida M. Seehafer (1948) lists usual residence as 509 Long Shore Dr (Unconfirmed match to 509 Cedar).
