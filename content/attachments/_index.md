# Attachments

## Folders
- `content/attachments/death-certificates/`: Michigan death certificate images used as primary sources.
- `content/attachments/directories/`: City directory PDFs.
- `content/attachments/aadl-directories/`: AADL directory screenshot excerpts (1902-1919).
- `content/attachments/military/`: WWI service record PDFs.
- `content/attachments/photos/`: Family photos and portraits.

## Notes
- Attachments are kept in their original formats; use the corresponding source pages under `content/sources/` for citations and transcriptions.
