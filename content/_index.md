## People

### Seehafer Family

The Seehafers immigrated from Germany and settled in Ann Arbor, Michigan. The family patriarch **William Gottlieb Seehafer** (1847-1921) and his wife **Wilhelmina Boelter** (1848-1922) raised their family at 509 Cedar Street (later Long Shore Drive).
